1.8:
- Added offline saving: Your progress (XP and time played) is now saved locally when offline and synced automatically when you go back online.
- Added option to link Google Account in Account Settings, allowing you to sign in with either your email or Google account. If you created your account with sign in with Google, this feature will not be available to you.
- Enhanced Leaderboard Sync: High scores now automatically sync between the in-game global leaderboard and Google Play Games.
- Privacy-focused Ranking: You will only appear on the Google Play Games leaderboard if you have a linked account, while still competing on the in-game global leaderboard.
- Updated offline banner to be more descriptive.
- Various bug fixes and stability improvements.
