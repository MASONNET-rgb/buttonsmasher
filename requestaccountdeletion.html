Request deletion of your data

Submit a verified request to delete your personal data from Button Smasher.

What deletion covers

  • Account identifiers: email/username associated with your Button Smasher account.
  • Gameplay & device data: progress, preferences, and telemetry tied to your account.
  • Support history: prior tickets and communications linked to your identity.

We may retain minimal records required for security, legal compliance, or fraud prevention.

Process and timeline

  • Verification: We confirm you own the account via email code or in-app sign-in.
  • Response: We acknowledge within 7 days and complete deletion within 30 days in most cases.
  • Confirmation: You’ll receive a confirmation once deletion is complete.

Policy and retention

We delete your personal data upon verified request, except where retention is required by law or for security and fraud prevention. Aggregate, non-identifiable analytics may be retained.
