Button Smasher — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-3-21

Short summary

Button Smasher collects the minimum personal data required to provide account functionality. This page explains what data we collect, why, how we use it, how long we keep it, with whom we share it, and how to contact us.

Data Controller

Controller: Button Smasher (the developer/publisher of the Button Smasher app). Contact: masonnet@masonnet.org.

What information we collect

We only collect the following information when you create an account in the app:

  • Username — chosen display name used within the app.
  • Email address — used for account verification, authentication, and account-related notifications (password reset, security alerts).
  • Device identifiers and IP address for moderation and ban enforcement — to prevent abuse and enforce bans, we may collect limited device identifiers and your current IP address when you use the app or when needed to investigate abuse. Examples include device model and operating system version; platform-specific device identifiers such as advertising IDs or other non-personally-identifying device tokens; and the IP address assigned to your device at the time of use. This information is collected only for moderation, abuse prevention, and ban enforcement and is not used for advertising or profiling.

We do not collect or process other personal data (for example: contacts, precise location, payment information, or usage analytics) unless you explicitly provide it in a support message or other voluntary communication.

How we use the information

  • To create and maintain your account and enable login.
  • To send account-related communications such as verification emails, password resets, and security notifications.
  • To prevent abuse and to troubleshoot and support the service. This includes using device identifiers and your current IP address to detect, investigate, and prevent abusive behavior, to enforce temporary or permanent bans, and to reduce ban evasion. Device identifiers and IP addresses are used only for these moderation and security purposes.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from public authorities when required.

Legal basis for processing (if applicable)

Where applicable, our lawful bases for processing are: performance of a contract (to provide the account and related features) and our legitimate interests (to secure the service and prevent abuse). For users in jurisdictions that require another legal basis, we will handle requests accordingly when contacted.

Data retention

We retain account information (username and email) for as long as your account exists. Device identifiers and IP addresses collected for moderation or ban enforcement are retained only as long as necessary to investigate and resolve the relevant abuse incident, to enforce bans, or to meet legal obligations. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within a reasonable time frame, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business reasons (e.g., to preserve evidence of abuse).

Data sharing and third parties

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. We may share the minimum necessary account data and moderation-related identifiers with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (for example, email delivery providers, abuse detection services, or identity verification services). We require such providers to process data only on our instructions and to maintain appropriate security controls. In limited circumstances, we may disclose device identifiers or IP addresses to law enforcement or other third parties when required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Button Smasher or others.

AI Services

Due to how our AI service operates, Button Smasher and its authorized service providers may access the content of in‑app AI conversations when necessary to deliver, maintain, secure, and troubleshoot the service (for example, for moderation, abuse prevention, and technical support). We do not sell or rent AI conversation content to third parties. We do not disclose AI conversation content for advertising purposes. Any internal use of AI conversation content is limited to the purposes described in this policy and to service operation, security, and quality assurance; retention and sharing are governed by the Data Retention and Data Sharing sections above.

International transfers

Your account data may be processed or stored in countries outside your own. When transferring data internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards as required by applicable law.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including access controls and encryption where appropriate. Device identifiers and IP addresses used for moderation are stored and accessed only by authorized personnel involved in security and trust operations. No system can be guaranteed fully secure; in the event of a security incident affecting your personal data, we will comply with applicable breach-notification laws.

Target audience and children

Button Smasher is intended for all age groups, including adults and minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, or if a parent or guardian asks us to delete such data, we will promptly delete the information upon receipt of a valid request sent to masonnet@masonnet.org.

Advertising and analytics

Button Smasher does not use advertising or collect analytics data by default. If we add analytics or advertising in the future, this policy will be updated and you will be informed of the change.

Choices and access

  • Access and correction — You can request access to or correction of your account information by contacting us.
  • Account deletion — You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us at the address below. We will honor valid requests subject to legal requirements.
  • Moderation identifiers — If you have questions about device identifiers or IP addresses collected for moderation, or if you believe such data is incorrect, contact us and we will review your request.
  • Marketing — We do not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in. You can opt out at any time.

How to contact us

For questions, requests to access, correct, or delete your data, or other privacy concerns, contact: masonnet@masonnet.org.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date shown at the top of this page and, where required, notify you by email or in-app notice.

This privacy policy is intended to be machine-readable and human-readable for app store review. If you need a machine-readable declaration specific to a region (for example, GDPR, CCPA disclosures, or a data processing addendum), contact us at masonnet@masonnet.org.
