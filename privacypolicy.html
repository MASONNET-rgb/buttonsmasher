Button Smasher — Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 11, 2025

Summary

Button Smasher collects a minimal set of personal information only when you create an account. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your choices.

Data we collect

We only collect the following information when you create an account:

  • Username — your chosen display name.
  • Email address — used for account verification and account-related notifications.

We do not collect any additional personal data, contact lists, device identifiers, location data, or payment information unless you voluntarily provide it in another context (e.g., support message).

How we use your data

  • Account creation and authentication.
  • Sending important account-related messages and notifications (for example, password resets or security alerts).
  • Basic abuse prevention and support diagnostics.

Data retention

We retain your username and email for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, we will remove your account information within a reasonable time frame unless otherwise required to retain data for legal or operational reasons.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include access controls and encryption where appropriate. No system is completely secure; if a security incident affects your personal information, we will follow applicable law for notification.

Third parties and sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share account data with trusted service providers who perform services on our behalf (for example, email delivery), and only to the extent necessary to provide those services. We require such providers to protect your data and use it only for authorized purposes.

Cookies and analytics

Button Smasher does not require cookies for account creation. If we use cookies or analytics tools in the future, this policy will be updated and you will be informed of the change.

Your choices

  • Access and correction — you can contact us to request access to or correction of your account information.
  • Account deletion — you can request deletion of your account and the associated data.
  • Marketing — we do not use your email for marketing unless you explicitly opt in; you can opt out at any time.

Children

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us to request deletion.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated policy and update the effective date above.

Contact

If you have questions or requests about this policy, please contact us at privacy@buttonsmasher.app
